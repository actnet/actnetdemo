清华大学计算机系知识工程研究室研究方向包括：语义Web、Web服务、数据挖掘、无线传感网、XML数据处理、软件生产 线、网构软件，已经在WWW、SIGMOD、ACL、SIGIR、ISWC、ICWS、TKDE等计算机领域国际重要的会议与期刊上发表了学术论 文，承担着国家自然科学基金、国家科技部973、863等科研项目的研究工作。此外，知识工程研究室将研究成果进行应用， 是国内最早将Java技术与XML技术应用到电子商务与电子政务应用中的单位，承担多项国际合作和国家部委级项目。
The Noah’s Ark Lab was founded in Hong Kong in 2012. Our basic mission is to conduct state-of-the-art research in data mining and artificial intelligence by exploring theories and building intelligent systems. Our goal is to push the frontier of our understanding on how knowledge can be learned and intelligence can be realized from the Big Data. We are the first large-scale industrial research lab in Hong Kong, and we aim to attract the best talents from the world. Our interests include natural language understanding and information retrieval, large-scale data mining and machine learning, social media and mobile intelligence, human-computer interaction and systems, as well as machine learning theory.
